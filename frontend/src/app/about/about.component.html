About Hand Hygiene Monitoring System ({{config.appName}})

H2MS is an application for keeping track of compliance activities in clinical environments, initially aimed at providing a low-cost solution to improve hand-hygiene compliance auditing in clinical settings, and ultimately to significantly reduce the prevalence of infections spread by hand-contacts. Hand hygiene monitoring can be performed through a human observer using web-form on mobile device, as well as automated recording through inexpensive microcontroller devices using RFID or Bluetooth tags on hospital personnel. In addition, the web-form can be easily modified by the administrator of the H2MS to adapt to other monitoring tasks, such as glove and mask compliance. Clinical staff can obtain feedback on their compliance rating for individual improvement, and compliance officers use the collected information to devise organizational improvement strategies.
